Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help a site function, remember preferences, and help us understand how it's used.

Cookies We Use

This site does not set any cookies of its own — no login, cart, or session state to track. The cookies that do get set all come from third-party tools we use to run the email opt-ins and video content, and only load after you accept:

  • MailerLite: our email marketing platform. Used both to power the opt-in forms and, once accepted, to recognize returning visitors and understand how people move through the site before subscribing.
  • Wistia: hosts the videos embedded on this site and sets cookies to track playback and viewing analytics.

Cloudflare Web Analytics: we use this to understand overall site traffic. It is cookie-free — it doesn't set any cookie or store anything on your device — so it isn't part of the accept/decline choice below. The limited data it processes (like IP address, handled transiently) is covered in the Privacy Policy above.

If you book a call through our scheduling popup, that action loads a Google Calendar page in an iframe, which may set its own cookies under Google's policies — this only happens if you actively open the booking popup.

We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies.

Your Choices

On pages with cookie-dependent content (videos, the newsletter signup), a popup lets you accept or decline the cookies listed above. If you decline, none of them are set — email opt-in forms still work, since submitting your email doesn't require MailerLite's tracking cookie to function. Accepting is remembered until you clear your browser's site data; declining (or closing the popup without choosing) means it will appear again on your next visit, so you can always change your mind. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings — note that blocking all cookies may affect website functionality.
