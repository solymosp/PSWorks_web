Why Firefighting Never Ends

Persistent firefighting is a vicious loop that is self-reinforcing: Long-impact daily work gets neglected, because efforts go into putting the more urgent fires out. This keeps the fires coming even more, until the management works its way out or sinks into heavy loss.

Catching this early requires wisdom, getting out of it requires prioritization.

This video is about a simple strategy for setting priorities so there is chance for long-term improvement before burnout happens.
