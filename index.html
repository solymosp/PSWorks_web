About Peter

Automotive Auditor & Hypnotherapist

Business problems are both systemic and human in origin. I've worked worldwide in quality and compliance management, and was entrusted to practise hypnotherapy as a personal commitment, so I've seen both sides.

Today I aim to serve this field with quality of education, review of individual cases, and consulting to design problem-solving systems that outperform the usual. My quest is to cut through the noise that plagues this field and to serve with clear, concise, actionable information, rooted in practice.

Problem-solving is one of the top value business skills, yet remains wildly undereducated. The rate of managers trained in structured problem solving worldwide can be estimated at around 10%. It is one of the most obvious sleeper differentiator for any manager who has no depth of experience and wants to outperform competition.
